About Us

Builder's Cranes supply self-erecting cranes for hire or sale to the Irish construction industry. We pride ourselves on our licensed and insured crane rental service giving you peace of mind.

With our well maintained modern crane fleet, we have the experience and equipment to accommodate your lifting needs efficiently and at a reasonable price.

Our safety procedures are fully compliant with up to date regulations.

What We Do

Self erect crane hire with sizing from 21m up to 40m and all types of accessories.


Inspections

Here at Builder's Cranes, we provide safety and compliance inspections on a wide arrange of equipment.

Preventative Maintenance

Prolong equipment longevity with scheduled and on-site preventative maintenance programs.

Breakdown & Repairs

Bring your equipment back to life with both emergency breakdown assistance and planned repairs.

Modernization

Increase productivity and efficiency with vital upgrades to your existing equipment.
